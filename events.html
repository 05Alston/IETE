2019

The Robowars Chapter#1 Sumo Wrestle Workshop: An IOT-ROBOT building workshop and competition was held. The aim was to learn about Arduino NANO Microcontroller and the Bluetooth HC-05 module, to make a robot interfaced with Arduino NANO module and to control the robot using a Bluetooth application. Circuit Simulation and PCB Designing workshop and competition was also conducted.

2017

A workshop on Node MCU ESP8266 IoT WiFi Module was conducted. The Valedictory programme of IETE Mumbai Oscillations 2017 was conducted at Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Mumbai to felicitate the winners of various events held at the ISF’s of IETE Mumbai spread across different engineering colleges in Mumbai under the banner of Oscillations 2017. Arduino Workshop to learn about Arduino Nano Module and to program the controller to interface the module was held. Circuit Simulation and PCB designing Workshop was held. 2016

The IETE-DBIT-ISF conducted Industrial Visit to Indian Institute of Geomagnetisim, Panvel. It also conducted workshop FUN WITH FM to develop a FM Transmitter using simple components such as transistors and LC circuit; workshop on Arduino to make students learn a new open-source project; workshop and competition on Line Follower to make students learn how to use a new open-source project for creating a Arduino based Line Follower Robot; workshop on Python. It also conducted a workshop on "Raspberry Pi: Basics, GPIO Interfacing and IOT" to program the GPIO pins of R-Pi for device status & control.
